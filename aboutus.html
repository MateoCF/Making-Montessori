About MetaMedia

MetaMedia is a drop-in digital media center and maker-education program designed for middle-school youth. The program was founded in January 2015 as a partnership between the McGaw YMCA, Youth & Opportunity United, and Northwestern University and has served over 1,400 middle-school youth. MetaMedia offers youth a hands-on, project-based, flexible after-school learning environment where they can develop strong relationships with peers and mentors.

About Chiaravalle Montessori DaVinci Studio

The DaVinci studio in the Chiaravalle North Wing is a STEAM-inspired maker space. STEAM is a movement championed by Rhode Island School of Design (RISD). Interdisciplinary learning is a prominent differentiator of Montessori education. The DaVinci studio will be a thoughtfully-designed space to showcase the Art + Science intersection.
